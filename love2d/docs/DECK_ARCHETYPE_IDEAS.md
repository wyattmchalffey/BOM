# New Deck Concepts (Beyond the Current 4 Factions)

Brief ideas for **new deck identities** that are not Human, Orc, Elf, or Gnome.

## 1) Tidebound Houses (Naval-Merchant Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Floating city-clans who weaponize trade routes, weather, and sea monsters.
- **Primary Resources:** Salt, Coin, Tide.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Flood tiles, convoy protection, blockade effects, and "harbor" structures.
- **Win Pattern:** Out-scale through trade engines, then drown board tempo with tide events.

## 2) Ashen Covenant (Volcanic Zealot Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Fanatics who believe destruction purifies the world.
- **Primary Resources:** Ember, Ash, Blood.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Self-burn for power, scorched zones, deathburst triggers.
- **Win Pattern:** Accelerates by sacrificing own board, then closes with chained wipe/burn turns.

## 3) Verdant Swarm (Insect Hive Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** A living hive-mind that spreads through brood and pheromone control.
- **Primary Resources:** Brood, Resin, Biomass.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Token multiplication, evolution tiers, queen aura effects.
- **Win Pattern:** Goes wide early, then mutates into overwhelming multi-lane pressure.

## 4) Iron Legion Remnant (Ancient Automaton Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Reactivated war-machines from a fallen prehistory.
- **Primary Resources:** Charge, Alloy, Core Fragments.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Overheat thresholds, stance swapping, modular chassis upgrades.
- **Win Pattern:** Survives early with armored shells and flips into unstoppable late mechs.

## 5) Umbral Court (Shadow Nobility Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Exiled aristocrats of dusk who weaponize secrets and fear.
- **Primary Resources:** Veil, Debt, Oaths.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Hidden information, forced bargains, delayed penalties.
- **Win Pattern:** Controls opponent options until they collapse under accumulating oaths/debts.

## 6) Sky Nomads (Storm-Rider Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Caravan tribes living on cliffs, kiteships, and lightning towers.
- **Primary Resources:** Wind, Spark, Ropework.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Mobility bonuses, lane-jumping units, storm counters.
- **Win Pattern:** Wins through constant repositioning and burst windows from storm buildup.

## 7) Grave Bloom Circle (Fungal Druid Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** A spore religion that sees death as fertile transition.
- **Primary Resources:** Spores, Rot, Nectar.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Infected death triggers, corpse gardens, delayed bloom effects.
- **Win Pattern:** Converts every casualty (ally or enemy) into incremental board advantage.

## 8) Glass Oracle Dominion (Crystal-Psionics Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Prophets who grow memory-crystals to rewrite probable futures.
- **Primary Resources:** Facets, Echoes, Focus.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Scry chains, prediction locks, copied spell lines.
- **Win Pattern:** Stabilizes with foresight, then executes deterministic combo turns.

## 9) Beast-King Clans (Primal Apex Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Warrior packs bonded to ancient megafauna bloodlines.
- **Primary Resources:** Hunt, Bone, Fury.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Mark/prey system, pack tactics, alpha ascension.
- **Win Pattern:** Picks priority targets, snowballs combat wins into alpha finishers.

## 10) Sunforged Theocracy (Radiant Law Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** A militant temple-state enforcing divine order through light.
- **Primary Resources:** Radiance, Verdict, Incense.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Judgment counters, purification zones, conditional exiles.
- **Win Pattern:** Locks out "unclean" enemy lines and wins through inevitability engines.

## 11) Wasteland Scavenger Union (Post-War Scrapper Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Scrap-barons and salvagers thriving in ruins between empires.
- **Primary Resources:** Scrap, Fuel, Salvage.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Rebuild from graveyard, improvised gear, random-but-upgradable rigs.
- **Win Pattern:** Turns destroyed cards into stronger second-life threats.

## 12) Dreaming Choir (Astral Bard Faction)
- **Core Fantasy:** Song-mystics who alter reality through layered harmonics.
- **Primary Resources:** Verse, Chorus, Reverie.
- **Signature Mechanics:** Combo "song chains," sleep/rest control, resonance amplifiers.
- **Win Pattern:** Survives through soft control, then ends games via high-resonance chain effects.
